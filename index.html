Biography

I am a Ph.D. student (2023-2028) jointly at the College of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence, Fudan University, and the Shanghai Innovation Institute, advised by Prof. Xipeng Qiu and Prof. Xuanjing Huang.

My current research focuses on AI Scientist, reinforcement learning, and multimodal reasoning.

I am always open to academic collaboration and discussion. Please feel free to contact me at jqtong25@m.fudan.edu.cn.
