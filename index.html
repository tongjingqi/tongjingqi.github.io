About

Research at the intersection of reasoning, learning, and evaluation.

My recent work studies how AI systems can reason across modalities, learn from scalable feedback signals, and be evaluated in realistic settings. The projects below cover video generation as a reasoning paradigm, reinforcement learning with game-based multimodal data, scientific taste learning from community feedback, and benchmarks for mathematical and medical reasoning.

This homepage is intentionally compact: it highlights selected work, keeps links close to each project, and leaves space for a formal CV, teaching, service, or personal notes when those are ready.
